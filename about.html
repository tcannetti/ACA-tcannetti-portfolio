Bio

Born in June 1990, I barely made it to Houston in time to call myself a Texan. My parents narrowly avoided giving birth to me in Florida, saving me from a lifetime of "Florida Man" jokes. I grew up in a sleepy suburb called, The Woodlands, before taking my talents to College Station.

I spent 5 years at Texas A&M Universtiy before graduating with a Bachelor of Liberal Arts in English Literature. In 2013 I moved to Austin, TX and worked in sales for 4 years before falling into a fun and exciting gig with my friends at The Austin Winery.
